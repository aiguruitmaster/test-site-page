Verification also protects your account from unauthorized access and ensures that withdrawals are processed securely to the rightful account owner.

What documents do I need for KYC verification?

To complete your verification at Bet Ninja Casino, you'll typically need to provide:

  • A government-issued photo ID (passport, driver's license, or national ID card)
  • Proof of address dated within the last three months (utility bill, bank statement, or official government correspondence)

Bet Ninja Casino strives to process verification documents as quickly as possible. Most accounts are verified within 24-48 hours of submitting complete and clear documentation.
